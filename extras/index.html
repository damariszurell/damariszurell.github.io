Damaris Zurell - Curriculum vitae

Contact - Education - Career - Projects - Awards - Professional activities - Memberships I am a professor of Ecology and Macroecology at the University of Potsdam, with a deep interest in how biodiversity responds to environmental change. My work bridges theoretical ecology, conservation science, and predictive biodiversity modelling, often connecting basic research with real-world conservation challenges. I lead an interdisciplinary research group, coordinate international initiatives, and actively contribute to global scientific networks on biodiversity and climate change.
